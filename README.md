🛒 Retail Sales Data Analysis
📌 Project Description

This project analyzes a retail sales dataset to extract meaningful business insights related to:

Customer purchasing behavior

Product category performance

Sales trends

The project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow, including:

Data cleaning and preprocessing using Python (Pandas)

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Data visualization using Matplotlib & Seaborn

Loading data into MySQL using SQLAlchemy

Writing SQL queries to answer business questions

Extracting actionable business insights

🎯 Objective

The goal of this project is to simulate how a Data Analyst helps businesses make data-driven decisions using transactional sales data.

📊 Data Visualizations & Insights using Matplotlib
1️⃣ Total Revenue by Product Category
🔎 Business Insight

This chart shows the total revenue generated by each product category.

Key Observations

Electronics generates the highest revenue (~157K)

Clothing follows closely (~156K)

Beauty generates slightly lower revenue (~144K)

Revenue is fairly balanced across all categories

💡 Business Implications

Electronics products likely have higher price points

Clothing products generate revenue through frequent purchases

Businesses should:

Maintain strong inventory for electronics

Promote clothing through seasonal campaigns

Bundle beauty products with clothing promotions

2️⃣ Monthly Sales Trend
Key Observations

May has the highest sales (~53K)

October and December also show strong performance

September records the lowest sales (~23K)

💡 Business Implications

Increase inventory before high demand months

Run discounts during slow months

Seasonal campaigns can maximize revenue

3️⃣ Distribution of Transaction Amount
Key Observations

Most purchases fall below 200

Smaller portion of transactions range 500–2000

Distribution is right-skewed

💡 Business Implications

Revenue depends heavily on frequent small purchases

Upselling and bundle deals can increase average order value

4️⃣ Revenue by Gender
Key Observations

Female customers generate ~232K revenue

Male customers generate ~223K revenue

💡 Business Implications

Both genders contribute significantly to revenue

Marketing should target both genders

5️⃣ Customer Age vs Spending
Key Observations

Customers aged 18–65 make purchases

No strong linear relationship between age and spending

Middle-aged customers often make higher value purchases

💡 Business Implications

Spending is not strongly dependent on age

Middle-aged customers are a key revenue segment

📊 Data Visualizations & Insights using Seaborn
1️⃣ Correlation Between Numeric Variables
Key Observations

Price per Unit ↔ Total Amount → Strong correlation (0.85)

Quantity ↔ Total Amount → Moderate correlation (0.37)

Age ↔ Spending → Very weak correlation

💡 Business Implications

Product pricing strongly impacts revenue

Premium products can increase total transaction value

2️⃣ Revenue Heatmap by Product Category and Gender
Key Observations

Clothing generates the highest revenue from female customers

Electronics generates the highest revenue from male customers

💡 Business Implications

Women prefer clothing

Men prefer electronics

Businesses can use gender-based marketing strategies

3️⃣ Product Category Preference by Age Group
Key Observations

Middle-aged customers generate highest revenue

Adults prefer clothing

Young customers buy more beauty products

💡 Business Implications

The 41-60 age group is the most valuable segment
